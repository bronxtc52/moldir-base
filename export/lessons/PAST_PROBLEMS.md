# Past Problems

Lessons carried into this repository from the Gateway pattern.

- Do not let generated answers invent facts outside the knowledge base.
- Do not mix active knowledge with historical archives.
- Do not make product claims sound like medical treatment.
- Do not treat prices in product descriptions as current if a more authoritative price file exists later.
- Do not expose internal IDs or source artifacts to end users unless the caller explicitly asks for technical provenance.
- Keep the active context small and readable: curated markdown in `export/knowledge`, machine indexes in `data`.
